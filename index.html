Always wanted to become an engineer, but didn't know how? Or maybe you don't know what kind of engineering you want to go into. Well, this is the right site for you! This is a comprehensive guide to engineering, including what they do, what universities you can go to and how to apply, and what the jobs are like!

Engineering is a great job to have, because you can start working right once you get out of school, and they pay a pretty good salary. In addition the world always needs more engineers, so don't worry about a shortage of jobs! Engineering is a stable job with plenty of job opportunities.

Photos by ThisisEngineering and Clint Bustrillos on Unsplash

This website will be focusing on five types of engineering: Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, and Biomedical Engineering. In addition, it will be focusing on three universities for each type: the University of British Columbia, the University of Waterloo, and the University of Toronto.

Please keep in mind that this information applied mostly to Canadians, especially those in BC.
